Return Policy

What can be returned?

  • Products must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging and tags must be intact
  • Shoes should show no signs of wear or damage
  • All accessories and documentation must be included

What cannot be returned?

  • Items purchased during final sale or clearance
  • Products that have been worn outdoors
  • Items with missing tags or packaging
  • Customized or personalized products

Return Window

You have 15 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return. Returns requested after this period cannot be accepted.

Exchange Policy

Size Exchange

If the size doesn't fit perfectly, we offer free size exchanges within 15 days of delivery.

  • Exchange for any available size of the same product
  • No additional shipping charges for size exchanges
  • One free exchange per order
  • Product must be unworn and in original condition

Color/Style Exchange

Want a different color or style? You can exchange your product, but shipping charges may apply. The product must be unused and in original packaging.

How to Return or Exchange

1

Login to Your Account

Go to "My Orders" in your account dashboard and select the order you want to return or exchange.

2

Request Return/Exchange

Click on "Return" or "Exchange" button, select the reason, and choose your preferred resolution.

3

Pack Your Item

Place the item in its original packaging with all tags and accessories. Use the return label we'll email you.

4

Ship It Back

Drop off your package at the nearest courier location. You'll receive tracking information via email.

5

Get Your Refund/Exchange

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days or ship your exchange.

Refund Information

Refund Method

Refunds will be credited to your original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 7-10 business days
  • Digital Wallet: 3-5 business days

Shipping Charges

Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item, defective product, etc.).
