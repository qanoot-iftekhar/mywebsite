How to Measure Your Feet

1

Prepare

Wear the socks you'll typically wear with your shoes. Stand on a piece of paper against a wall.

2

Trace

With a pen held vertically, trace the outline of your foot, keeping the pen close to your foot.

3

Measure

Measure the distance from your heel to your longest toe. This is your foot length.

4

Compare

Match your measurement (in cm) with our size chart above to find your perfect size.

Fit Guide

Narrow Fit

If you have narrow feet, we recommend going down half a size for a snugger fit. Our shoes run true to size but may feel loose on narrow feet.

Regular Fit

Our shoes are designed for regular width feet. Order your normal size for the perfect fit.

Wide Fit

If you have wide feet, we recommend going up half a size. This will provide extra room for comfort.

Sizing Tips

  • Always measure both feet - use the larger measurement if they differ
  • Measure your feet at the end of the day when they're at their largest
  • If between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort
  • Check customer reviews for fit feedback on specific products
  • Still unsure? Contact our customer service team for personalized help
